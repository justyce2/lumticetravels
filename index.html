What We Do

We are in the travel Business, We assist our client to study in choice universities across the worlds, Provide Visa Assitance for Business, Work and tourism as well as travel Insurance, Flights and hotel booking.

OVERSEA EDUCATION ASSISTANCE

We are official representatives of a wide range of universities & colleges worldwide. We offer study abroad services to our prospective clients with a wide range of study destinations from United States, Canada, UK/Europe, Australia, and more.

VISA ASSITANCE

We provide professional assistance for your visa processing. We help clients with legal documents & invitation letters to make the travel process smooth and increase the chances of visa approval, be it student visa, visiting visa, tour, business or work.

FLIGHT AND HOTEL BOOKING

We have a robust flight online booking system which gives us an edge at booking local and international flights at competitive prices. We provide travel advisory services and a structure travel itinerary for clients according to interests.
